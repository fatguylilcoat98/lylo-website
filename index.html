Just A Regular Guy Who Cares

"I’m not a tech CEO from Silicon Valley, and I don’t have a fancy computer science degree. I’m just a regular guy who got tired of seeing good people get taken advantage of."

I’ve been scammed myself, and I’ve watched my own family members lose money to people who prey on confusion. I’ve always been the one my elderly relatives call to fix their phones or explain a weird email. Through helping them, I realized something painful: technology is moving too fast, and the people who need protection the most—our seniors and those with disabilities—are being left behind.

I built this tool to be the bridge. That is why I named it LYLO: Love Your Loved Ones. My goal is to make sure that no matter your age or ability, you have someone in your corner protecting you.

No one left behind.
