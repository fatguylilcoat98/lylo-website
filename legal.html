Effective Date: February 2026

At LYLO, we build digital bodyguards, not surveillance networks. Our privacy architecture is designed under a strict "Zero Human Access" protocol.

1. Zero Human Access & Log Purging

Your conversations with LYLO experts are processed programmatically. We do not retain human-readable chat logs for our engineers or staff to review. Data processed during your session is strictly used to generate your immediate response and update your encrypted vector memory.

2. 0% AI Model Training

We absolutely never use your personal conversations, financial data, uploaded photos, or identifying markers to train foundational AI models (like OpenAI or Google Gemini). Your data remains isolated within your personal session environment.

3. Financial Information

LYLO does not store or process your credit card information. All payments and subscriptions are handled securely through Stripe, a certified PCI Level 1 Service Provider. We only store an encrypted token verifying your active subscription tier.

If you utilize LYLO's voice capabilities, the microphone strictly processes audio locally in your browser. Audio streams are transcribed and instantly discarded. LYLO employs an automatic 120-second standby cutoff to ensure your ambient environment is never inadvertently recorded.
