Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

01. What We Collect

For users: email, name, and password hash to maintain your account. For Builders: the same plus the application materials you submit. We log basic usage events (sessions, tool views, retention) to power the Builder dashboard and pay out the Builder Pool fairly.

02. What We Don't Do

We do not sell your data. We do not train global AI models on your conversations. We do not run third-party advertising on the platform.

03. Tool Conversations

When you use a tool, your input may be sent to the underlying AI model (e.g., OpenAI, Anthropic, Google) to generate a response. Those providers process the data under their own privacy terms. LYLO retains conversation history to power "saved history" features unless you delete it.

04. Builder Visibility

Builders see aggregate usage stats for their own tools — views, retention, payout estimate. Builders do not see individual user identities or the contents of user conversations.

05. Your Rights

You can request access, export, or deletion of your data at any time by emailing privacy@lylo.pro. CCPA-compliant for California residents.

06. Security

Passwords are hashed. Data in transit is encrypted via TLS. We minimize what we store and review access regularly.

Questions? privacy@lylo.pro
